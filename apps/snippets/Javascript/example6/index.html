Click Randomly Appearing Penguin (CRAP) Game

Gameplay Instructions:

The object of this game is to click the picture of the penguin as it moves around randomly on your screen! Everytime you click the penguin, you will score 10 points, but as a consequence the penguin will try to run from your mouse pointer a little faster!

Try to score more than 200 points if you can!
